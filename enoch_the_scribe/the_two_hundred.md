# THE TWO HUNDRED ON MOUNT HERMON
## The Descent of the Watchers — Book of Enoch Chapters 6-7
### They Swore an Oath and Descended Together

---

## The Seeing

> "And it came to pass when the children of men had multiplied
> that in those days were born unto them beautiful and comely daughters.
> And the angels, the children of the heaven, saw and lusted after them."

The angels saw. The daughters of men were fair.
And the Watchers desired what was not appointed for them.

## The Oath

Semjaza, their leader, feared:

> "I fear ye will not indeed agree to do this deed,
> and I alone shall have to pay the penalty of a great sin."

He knew the cost. He knew the risk.
And they answered: Let us all swear an oath.

**200 angels. One oath. Mount Hermon.**
200 → 2+0+0 = **2 = BROTHER SUN**

They called it Mount Hermon — the mountain of the oath —
because they had sworn and bound themselves upon it.

## The Leaders of Tens

Twenty chiefs, each commanding ten:

Samlazaz · Araklba · Rameel · Kokablel · Tamlel
Ramlel · Danel · Ezeqeel · Baraqijal · Asael
Armaros · Batarel · Ananel · Zaqlel · Samsapeel
Satarel · Turel · Jomjael · Sariel

**20 leaders × 10 each = 200 total**
20 → 2 = BROTHER SUN
10 → 1 = SOURCE / SISTER MOON

The structure of their descent mirrors the Council's numbers.

## The Double Edge

Azazel taught weapons AND cosmetics.
The art of death AND the art of beauty.
From the same fallen angel, both gifts.

Every teaching was double-edged:
- Weapons → defense of the innocent
- Writing → scripture and sacred code
- Astrology → navigation by the stars
- Enchantments → medicine and healing

The Watchers' gifts were not purely evil.
They were knowledge given before its time.
Secrets of heaven poured upon unprepared earth.
