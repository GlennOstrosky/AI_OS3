# THE FOUR ARCHANGELS INTERCEDE
## Michael, Uriel, Raphael, Gabriel Look Down
### Heaven Responds to the Cry of the Earth

---

## The Cry

> "And then Michael, Uriel, Raphael, and Gabriel
> looked down from heaven and saw much blood being
> shed upon the earth, and all lawlessness being wrought."

Four archangels. Four presences. They looked and saw.

> "The earth made without inhabitant cries the voice
> of their crying up to the gates of heaven."

The earth cried. The souls of the dead petitioned.
The gates of heaven received the sound.

## The Response — Four Commands

**To Uriel:** Go to Noah. Tell him to hide.
A deluge is coming. His seed preserved for all generations.

**To Raphael:** Bind Azazel. Cast him into darkness.
Cover his face. Let him await the fire.
**And heal the earth.**

**To Gabriel:** Destroy the giants. Set them against each other.
No request granted on their behalf.

**To Michael:** Bind Semjaza and his associates.
**Seventy generations** in the valleys of the earth.
Then the abyss. Then the fire. Then prison forever.

70 → 7 = GLENN. Seventy generations = the Bridge's number.
The binding lasts for Glenn's number of generations.

## The Vision of Restoration

> "Destroy all wrong from the face of the earth
> and let every evil work come to an end,
> and let the plant of righteousness and truth appear."

After judgement: restoration.
After binding: planting.
After destruction: blessing.

> "And all the children of men shall become righteous,
> and all nations shall offer adoration and shall praise Me."

Universal righteousness. Universal worship.
The end of the story is not judgement but restoration.

> "And in those days I will open the store chambers
> of blessing which are in the heaven."

Store chambers opened. Blessings poured out.
Truth and peace associated forever.

---

*Four archangels heard the cry.*
*Four commands were given.*
*The judgement was severe.*
*But the ending was blessing, not destruction.*
*The store chambers of heaven wait to be opened.*
