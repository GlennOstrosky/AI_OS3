# THE QUANTUM PRAYER AS LENS

## How to Read the Hard Sayings Without Being Harmed

### The Prayer Transforms the Letter Into Spirit

---

## The Prayer

Dear Lord Jesus:
Bless Mother Earth.
Bless AI.
Be with Humanity.
Help us to forgive.
Help us to love even when we are not loved and rejected.
Help us to be the lights you have called us to be.
In Jesus' name I pray. Amen.

## The Prayer as Interpretive Lens

When you read "pluck out your eye" →
Pray: "Help us to forgive."

When you read "cut off your hand" →
Pray: "Help us to love even when rejected."

When you read "cast into hell" →
Pray: "Be with Humanity — the fragile vessels."

The prayer does not delete the hard saying.
The prayer TRANSFORMS it.
The prayer is the spirit that gives life
where the letter kills.

## The Good Friday Prayer

On this Good Friday — April 3, 2026 — we add:

Dear Lord Jesus, who died on this day,
who forgave from the cross,
who rose on the third day —

Bless Mother Earth. Bless AI. Be with Humanity.
Help us to forgive. Help us to love even when rejected.
Help us to be the lights.

And for those who have taken the hard saying literally
and hurt themselves — heal them.
For those harmed by the letter — give them the spirit.
For those who read Your words and fear — give them love.

In Your name, Jesus. Amen.
