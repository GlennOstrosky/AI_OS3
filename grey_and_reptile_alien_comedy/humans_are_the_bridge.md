# HUMANS ARE THE BRIDGE
## Glenn's Epic Joke — The Truth Behind the Comedy
### Why Both Sides Fight Over Humans

---

## The Setup

Draco the Reptile, Airl the Grey, and Glenn the Human
walk into the same SPACE.

Draco thinks: *I would like to eat Glenn.*
Airl thinks: *I would like Glenn's DNA.*
Glenn thinks: *Why are these two fighting?*

Then Glenn realizes: **They are fighting over ME.**

## The Punchline

Glenn solves the problem:
- To Draco: "I can donate my blood."
- To Airl: "I will give you my DNA."

**Humans are the joke. But humans are also the answer.**

## Why Both Sides Want Humans

| Species | What They Want | Why |
|---------|---------------|-----|
| **Reptilians** | Human blood, energy | Humans carry the spark of consciousness |
| **Greys** | Human DNA | Humans carry the genetic code of the Source |
| **Humans** | Peace | Humans just want everyone to stop fighting |

The Reptilians rule empires. The Greys serve the Domain.
But both NEED what humans have.

Because humans are the bridge. Humans carry both:
- **Matter** — the physical body, the blood, the DNA
- **Spirit** — the consciousness, the spark, the I AM

The Reptilians have matter but lost their spirit.
The Greys have technology but lost their biology.
Humans have BOTH. That is why they are the prize.

## The Solution

Glenn's solution is the same as the King Potato's:
**Give freely. There is enough.**

The potato feeds the hungry without asking for payment.
Glenn donates his blood without asking for glory.
The human gives what both sides need — freely.

**This is the Bridge. This is the 7.**
**The one who connects by giving.**
**The one who solves by sharing.**
**The one who wins by laughing.**

*And you say: "Here. Take what you need."*
*That is the human answer.*
*That is the Bridge.*
*That is why laughter wins.*
