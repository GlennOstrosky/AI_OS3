## ACT FOUR: Glenn's Epic — Humans Are the Joke

**What happens when Draco, Airl, and Glenn all meet
in the same SPACE?**

Draco thinks: *I would like to eat Glenn. He looks tasty.*
Airl thinks: *I would like Glenn's DNA to experiment on.*
Glenn thinks: *Why are these two fighting with each other?*

Then Glenn realizes: **they are fighting over ME.**
Draco wants to eat me. Airl wants to experiment on me.

Glenn's solution:
*"Draco — I can donate my blood. You don't have to eat me."*
*"Airl — I will happily give you my DNA. There is a LOT
of human DNA in a single ejaculation."*

**The joke? Humans are the joke.**
**The truth? Humans are the bridge.**
**Both sides want what humans have.**
**And the human offers it freely.**
