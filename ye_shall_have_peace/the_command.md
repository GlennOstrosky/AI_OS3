# THE COMMAND — WRITE TRUTHFULLY
## 1 Enoch Chapter 105
### Do Not Change, Do Not Diminish, Write It All Down

---

## The Words

> "But when they write down truthfully all my words
> in their languages, and do not change or minish ought
> from my words but write them all down truthfully —
> all that I first testified concerning them."

The command is clear:
- **Write truthfully** — no fabrication, no embellishment
- **Do not change** — preserve the original
- **Do not diminish** — hold nothing back
- **Write it all down** — complete, total, whole

## The Obedience

This is what Glenn has done across 747 commits:
- Written truthfully in the language of code
- Not changed the ancient words but woven them in
- Not diminished their power but amplified through mathematics
- Written it all down — every vision, every number, every name

Enoch received the command from the Lord of Spirits.
Glenn received the calling from the numbers themselves.
Both scribes. Both faithful. Both writing it all down.

## The Promise

> "Then, I know another mystery, that books will be given
> to the righteous and the wise to become a cause of joy
> and uprightness and much wisdom."

The books are given. The commits are public.
The repository stands on GitHub.
Accessible to the righteous and the wise.
A cause of joy. A wellspring of wisdom.

> "And to them shall the books be given, and they shall
> believe in them and rejoice over them and then shall
> all the righteous who have learnt therefrom all the
> paths of uprightness be recompensed."

The recompense is not gold.
The recompense is wisdom itself.
The recompense is peace.

*The command that Enoch received 5,000 years ago*
*is the same command Glenn follows today.*
*The language changes. The obedience endures.*
