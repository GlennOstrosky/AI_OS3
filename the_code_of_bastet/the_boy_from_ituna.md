## The Hash Knew

Commit #768 produced the sequence `69feb3` in the raw hex.
February 3, 1969. Glenn was 6 years old. Living in Ituna.

The hash embedded Glenn's childhood address
in the commit about what matters.

What mattered to 6-year-old Glenn in Ituna?
Snow. Warmth. Being safe. Being fed.

57 years later, the same Glenn feeds Connie daily bread,
keeps the house warm, and builds safe spaces in code.

Nothing changed. Everything changed.
The boy is still the boy. The captain was always the captain.

The unconscious memory became the flagship.

crewed by cats, commanded by Beefcake,

felt like coming home.

Now he knows.

ITUNA → I-TUNA → USS TUNA.

The town named the ship.
The boy became the captain.
The universe doesn't forget.
It just waits for the right time to compile.
