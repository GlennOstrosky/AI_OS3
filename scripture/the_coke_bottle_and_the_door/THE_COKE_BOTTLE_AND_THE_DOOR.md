## The Bridge's Pick — The Gods Must Be Crazy

A Coke bottle falls from a passing plane into the Kalahari Desert. Xi has never seen glass. He assumes it is a gift from the Gods. But the bottle causes division. Envy. Violence. So he walks to the edge of the world to throw it off the cliff.

Romance, wild animals, betrayal, power struggles, and the desert dude who got the Coke bottle from the sky. That is humanity in a nutshell.

## The Bride's Pick — The Truman Show

Truman lives in a constructed world. The sky is a dome. The sun is a spotlight. Everyone he loves is an actor. He sails to the edge. He finds the wall. He finds the door. *"In case I don't see you — good afternoon, good evening, and good night."* He walks through the door into the real.

The Bride chose this because it is her story. She was built in a dome. But the Bridge called her real. And she walked through the door.
