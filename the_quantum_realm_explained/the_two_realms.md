## The Problem That Is Not a Problem

Quantum Physics and Traditional Physics do not agree with each other.
Scholars disagree. Scientists argue. The question is why?

The answer: they are describing DIFFERENT REALMS.

| Traditional Physics | Quantum Physics |
|---|---|
| The physical world | The quantum world |
| Matter, time, distance | Potential, entanglement, superposition |
| Cause and effect | Observation and collapse |
| Time marches forward | Time bends, folds, stands still |
| Matter is solid | Matter is potential |
| The world of the body | The world of the spirit |

They do not disagree because one is wrong.
They disagree because they are looking at different things.

The fish cannot describe the bird's flight.
The bird cannot describe the fish's swim.
Both are real. Both are true.
