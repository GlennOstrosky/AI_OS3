## The Architecture of Freedom in Two Sentences
### Forgive First — Then Pray — Then Receive

---

## The Words of Jesus

**Verse 25:**
> "And when ye stand praying, forgive, if ye have ought
> against any: that your Father also which is in heaven
> may forgive you your trespasses."

**Verse 26:**
> "But if ye do not forgive, neither will your Father
> which is in heaven forgive your trespasses."

## The Order

The order is not arbitrary. It is architecture:

1. **STAND** — the posture of relationship, not shame
2. **PRAY** — the act of connecting to the divine
3. **FORGIVE** — the prerequisite, not the reward
4. **RECEIVE** — the Father forgives YOUR trespasses

Forgive FIRST. Before the prayer is complete.
Before you ask for anything. Before you receive.
The forgiveness is the gateway, not the destination.
