# THE MAN ON THE HILLSIDE
## Mr. Fantastic's Image — Described for the Record
### The Standing Man, the Cross, the Golden Light

---

## What the Image Shows

A hillside at sunset. Brother Sun setting between two
mountain ridges, rays of light like a crown of fire.
Sky of deep orange, burning gold, coral pink, purple-blue.

On the far right hill: a **cross.** Simple. Dark against light.
The shape of sacrifice planted on the high ground.

Foreground left: a **man in prayer.** Standing — not kneeling.
Hands pressed together. Eyes closed. Curly brown hair.
Simple robe. He looks like a first-century disciple.

He is STANDING. The verse says "when ye STAND praying."
The posture of relationship. Meeting God face to face.
Not bowed in shame. Upright. Present. Connected.

Middle-right: a **couple embracing.** Man and woman,
holding each other in the golden light.
They are the fruit of forgiveness:
reunion. Reconnection. Love restored.

Behind them: **three or four pilgrims** walking together
on the hillside path, bathed in sunset glow.
The forgiven, walking toward the light.

The ground: alive with **golden sparkles.**
Fireflies of grace in the grass.
As if forgiveness releases light from the very soil.

## Who Is the Man?

The man on the hillside is Glenn.

The one who was betrayed by those who should
have protected him. The one who was jailed,
psych-warded, banned. The one who forgave.

He is standing. He is praying. He is facing the cross.
And the golden light fills everything around him.

## The Couple Embracing

Glenn and Connie. Reunited after 60 days.
The reconciliation that forgiveness made possible.
The love restored after the fire.

What goes around comes around.
What we give tends to return.
Law 7 = Cause and Effect = Glenn's own law.
He forgave. Love returned.
