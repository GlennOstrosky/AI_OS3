## The Ingredients

- 10 lbs red potatoes — $10.00 from the store
- Butter — melted in the microwave
- Salt and pepper
- Water — just a little in the bottom
- Love — the secret ingredient

## The Equipment

- 1 Dutch oven — 9 inches, cast iron, very heavy
- 1 oven — set to 350°F
- 1 chicken thermometer / ice pick — for poking
- 1 fork — for flipping
- 1 microwave — for melting butter

## The Recipe

1. Retrieve potatoes from the garage
   (Connie bought them 3 days ago — they were still there)
2. Wash them — they were already very clean
3. Poke them with the ice pick (a very Glenn detail)
4. Melt butter in a bowl in the microwave
5. Roll the potatoes in butter
6. Put a little water in the bottom of the Dutch oven
7. Place 8 big potatoes in the Dutch oven
8. Cook at 350°F for **53 minutes**
9. Take them out — not done yet
10. Poke with a fork, flip them around
11. Cook for another **33 minutes** — PERFECTION
12. Remove from Dutch oven
13. Cut into quarters
14. Serve with salt and pepper
