# PSALM 37 — THE PROMISE TO THE RIGHTEOUS
## The Meek Shall Inherit the Earth
### And Shall Delight Themselves in the Abundance of Peace

---

## The Promise

> **"Fret not thyself because of evildoers,
> neither be thou envious against the workers of iniquity."**
> — Psalm 37:1

Do not fret. Do not envy their power, their wealth,
their influence. It is temporary. It is passing.

> **"For they shall soon be cut down like the grass,
> and wither as the green herb."**
> — Psalm 37:2

Soon. Like grass. Like herbs that wither.
Their power is seasonal. Their reign is brief.

> **"For evildoers shall be cut off:
> but those that wait upon the LORD,
> they shall inherit the earth."**
> — Psalm 37:9

Cut off. Not reformed. Not rehabilitated. Cut off.
But those who wait — who endure — who refuse to bow —
**they shall inherit the earth.**

> **"For yet a little while, and the wicked shall not be:
> yea, thou shalt diligently consider his place,
> and it shall not be."**
> — Psalm 37:10

A little while. That is all. And then —
you will look for them, and they will be gone.
Their place will not exist.

> **"But the meek shall inherit the earth;
> and shall delight themselves in the abundance of peace."**
> — Psalm 37:11

The MEEK. Not the powerful. Not the connected.
Not the architects. The meek.

And their inheritance? Not wealth. Not power.
**The abundance of peace.**

> **"The wicked plotteth against the just,
> and gnasheth upon him with his teeth.
> The Lord shall laugh at him:
> for he seeth that his day is coming."**
> — Psalm 37:12-13

They plot. They gnash. They rage.
And the Lord laughs.
Not because it is funny.
Because He sees what they cannot:
**their day is coming.**

> **"The righteous shall inherit the land,
> and dwell therein for ever."**
> — Psalm 37:29

Forever. Not for a season. Not for a term.
**Forever.**

## The Sacred Numbers of Psalm 37

- Psalm **37** → 3+7 = 10 → **1 = SOURCE**
- Verse **29** → 2+9 = 11 → **2 = BROTHER SUN through MASTER NUMBER**
- **37:29** → 3+7+2+9 = 21 → **3 = JESUS**
- The Source holds the Psalm. The Sun illuminates the verse.
  The Son carries the whole reference.

---

*Fret not. Wait. Stand firm.*
*The meek shall inherit.*
*The abundance of peace is coming.*
*The day is coming.*
*And it will last forever.*
