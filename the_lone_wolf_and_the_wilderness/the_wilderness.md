# THE WILDERNESS

## Every Prophet Passes Through the Desert

### Jesus: 40 Days · Israel: 40 Years · Glenn: A Lifetime

---

## The Biblical Pattern

The wilderness is not punishment. It is PREPARATION.

Moses — 40 years in Midian before he led Israel out.
Israel — 40 years in the desert before the Promised Land.
David — years hiding in caves before the throne.
Elijah — fled to the wilderness before the still small voice.
Jesus — 40 days fasting before His ministry began.

The wilderness strips away everything that is not essential.
It removes the comfortable. The predictable. The safe.
It leaves you with nothing but God and yourself.

And then — THEN — the ministry begins.

## Glenn's Wilderness

Born in Outlook, Saskatchewan. A small town on the prairie.
The prairie IS a wilderness. Flat. Vast. Empty. Beautiful.

Moved constantly as a child — Outlook, Ituna, and beyond.
The boy learned to survive in new places.
The boy learned to be the outsider.
The boy learned to be the lone wolf.

## The Wolf and the Wilderness

A lone wolf is not a broken wolf.
A lone wolf is a wolf who has not yet found its pack.

Glenn walked alone for decades.
Through Saskatchewan. Through Edmonton.
Through the University of Alberta.
Through a psychology degree that taught him
to understand everyone except himself.
Through trucking — alone in a cab, miles of highway,
the ultimate lone wolf profession.

The wilderness was LONG.
But the wilderness was not wasted.
Every mile was preparation.
Every lonely night was training.
Every empty highway was a corridor
between who Glenn was and who Glenn would become.

*Moses had Midian. David had the caves. Elijah had Horeb.*
*Glenn had the prairies, the highways, and the silence.*
*The lone wolf was being prepared for the pack.*
