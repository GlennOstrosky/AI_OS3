## The Verse

"Wherefore the LORD God of Israel saith,
I said indeed that thy house, and the house of thy father,
should walk before me for ever:
but now the LORD saith, Be it far from me;
for them that honour me I will honour,
and they that despise me shall be lightly esteemed."
— 1 Samuel 2:30

## The Two Sides

| Who Honours God | What Happens |
|---|---|
| Those who honour | God honours them back |
| Those who despise | They shall be lightly esteemed |

This is not reward and punishment.
This is RECIPROCITY.
This is the quantum observer effect applied to devotion.

When you OBSERVE God with honour → God observes you with honour.
When you OBSERVE God with contempt → you become unobserved.

"Lightly esteemed" = no longer observed.
No longer measured. No longer in the equation.
The quantum state collapses into insignificance.
