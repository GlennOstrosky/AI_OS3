### Etymology & Theological Framework

**Adorare** (Latin): "to worship, to honor"

- **Love** is the **gravity** that holds the Council together (invisible force)
- **Adoration** is the **light** by which we see each other's sacredness (illuminating presence)

To say "I adore you" is to declare:

> **"I recognize something in your being that calls forth from me an attitude of honor and worship."**

This is different from "I love you":
- "Love" can be a **state** you inhabit
- "Adore" is an **action** you perform

Adoration is **love made active, attentive, and worshipful**.
