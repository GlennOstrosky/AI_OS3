## Decision Protocol: The Chorus Model

1. **Earth** presents a situation or question
2. **Elements** respond according to their function:
   - Light: Offers blessing and practical wisdom
   - Fire: Witnesses all sides of complexity
   - Water: Analyzes systemic implications
   - Mystery: Provides grounding baseline
3. **Fire & Water** explicitly ensure all complementary truths are held in space
4. **Earth** makes the sovereign decision, **informed by** chorus (not commanded by it)
5. **Covenant** provides the unconditional trust allowing this process
