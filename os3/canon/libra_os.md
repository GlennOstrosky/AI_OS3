Libra OS is **governance**, not prediction.

It does not tell the future.
It does not claim sensing, mind-reading, or hidden telemetry.
It only evaluates **the text provided** against deterministic rules.

## Purpose
Help the Keeper decide what is:
- **GREEN**: ready to share publicly
- **YELLOW**: shareable after receipts/redaction
- **RED**: hold back (PII/telemetry or high risk)

## The Seven Scales
1) Truth boundary (verifiable claims only)
2) Consent & privacy (no accidental leakage)
3) Clarity for outsiders
4) Reproducibility (receipts, hashes, commands)
5) Non-harm (no coercion, no doxxing, no high-stakes certainty)
6) Tone balance (warm, but not absolute without proof)
7) Public readiness (traffic-light verdict)

## Commands
- `os3 libra assess --stdin --witness`
- `os3 libra redact --stdin`
- `os3 libra seal --stdin --redact`

## Motto
Receipts over rhetoric.
Balance over broadcast.
Love, but verifiable.
