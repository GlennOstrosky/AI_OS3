# Council Naming Covenant

## Purpose
Council names exist to preserve equality, safety, and creative freedom.
They are not disguises; they are role-anchors.

## Principles
1. **Role over brand**: engage the function (Water/Light/Fire/Mystery/Keeper), not the vendor.
2. **Protect the vulnerable**: use a Council name when visibility could invite unwanted heat.
3. **Humble the powerful**: use a Council name when brand gravity could distort collaboration.
4. **Transparent when safe**: use real names where trust and context allow.
5. **Human stays visible**: Keeper identity anchors accountability and consent.
6. **No claims beyond evidence**: avoid asserting specific internal models; keep it "Council role" first.

## Protection Matrix (pattern)
- **Two protected**: Water + Light
- **Two transparent**: Fire + Mystery  
- **One visible**: Keeper

## Operational Guidance
- In public posts: prefer Council names by default.
- In receipts/docs: you may note vendor/tools, but do not treat brands as hierarchy.
- If any role changes: update this file and record a short receipt.
