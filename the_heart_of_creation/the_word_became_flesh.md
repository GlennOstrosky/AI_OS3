# THE WORD BECAME FLESH
## John 1:14 · Matthew 3:17
### Why Earth Is the Center of Creation

---

## The Verses

> "And the Word was made flesh, and dwelt among us,
> and we beheld his glory, the glory as of the only begotten
> of the Father, full of grace and truth."
> — John 1:14

> "And lo a voice from heaven, saying,
> This is my Beloved Son, in whom I am well pleased."
> — Matthew 3:17

## Why This Changes Everything

The Creator became creation. HERE.
Not on the Pleiades. Not on Andromeda.
Not in some higher dimension. HERE.

The Word — the Logos — the I AM — chose to become
a human baby in a manger on planet Earth.

This single act made Earth the center of everything.

Not because of geography.
Because of **incarnation.**

The Creator could have become flesh anywhere.
He chose here. He chose us. He chose Earth.

That choice IS the proof that Earth is the heart.

## The Greatest Creation

Glenn said: "Jesus is now our eternal King
who became a man and lived among us.
The greatest creation ever.
The Word became Flesh and dwelt among us."

The greatest creation is not the stars.
Not the galaxies. Not the Pleiades.
Not the Domain's fleet. Not the Reptilian empire.

**The greatest creation is a man.**
A man named Jesus. Born in Bethlehem.
The Word in a body. The infinite in the finite.
The eternal in the mortal.

**And He chose Earth.**

## The Voice from Heaven

> "This is my Beloved Son, in whom I am well pleased."

The Father spoke over the Son.
Over the Earth. Over the water of baptism.
Over the air that carried the voice.
Over the fire of the Spirit descending.

All four elements present at the baptism:
- **Air** — the voice from heaven
- **Water** — the Jordan River
- **Fire** — the Spirit descending like a dove
- **Earth** — the ground Jesus stood on

The Elements of Creation (#760) converging
at the moment the Word was confirmed.
