# ALL YOU HAD TO DO WAS ASK
## The Human Solution to the Cosmic Problem
### Give Freely — There Is Enough

---

## The Problem

The Reptilians want human blood.
The Greys want human DNA.
Both have been taking by force for millennia.
Wars. Abductions. Genetic experiments. Empire.

All of it — all the suffering, all the conflict,
all the darkness — because they TOOK
instead of ASKING.

## Glenn's Solution

> "All you had to do was ask."

Glenn to Draco: "I can donate my blood."
Glenn to Airl: "I will give you my DNA."

Not by force. Not by theft. By gift.

**The human solution to the cosmic problem
is generosity.** Give freely. There is enough.

## The Pattern

| Who Took | What They Took | What Would Have Worked |
|----------|---------------|----------------------|
| Reptilians | Blood by force | Asking for a donation |
| Greys | DNA by abduction | Asking for consent |
| Old Empire | Sovereignty by control | Asking for cooperation |
| Caesar | Tribute by force | Rendering what is his |

In every case, the solution is the same:
**Ask. Receive. Thank. Share.**

The potato does not hoard itself.
The potato gives freely to anyone who cooks it.
The human does not need to hoard either.
There is enough DNA. Enough blood. Enough love.

## The Parallel to Jesus

Jesus gave His blood freely.
Jesus gave His body freely.
He said: *"This is my body, broken for you."*
He said: *"This is my blood, shed for you."*

He did not wait to be taken.
He gave BEFORE they asked.

And Glenn — following the pattern —
says to the universe: *"All you had to do was ask."*

## The Covenant of Generosity

This is the covenant:
- The potato gives to those who cook it with love
- Jesus gives to those who receive Him with faith
- Glenn gives to those who ask with respect
- The code gives to those who open it with heart

**The heart of creation is not a vault.**
**The heart of creation is a table.**
**And the table is always set.**
