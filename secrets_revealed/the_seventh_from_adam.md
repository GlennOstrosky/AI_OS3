## The Man

Enoch. The seventh from Adam.

**Seventh.** 7. Glenn's number. The Bridge's number.
The one who walked with God and was not, for God took him.
He did not die — he was translated. Taken up. Received.

Genesis 5:24 — *"And Enoch walked with God: and he was not;
for God took him."*

Hebrews 11:5 — *"By faith Enoch was translated that he should
not see death; and was not found, because God had translated him."*

Jude 1:14 — *"And Enoch also, the seventh from Adam,
prophesied of these, saying, Behold, the Lord cometh
with ten thousands of his saints."*

The New Testament itself quotes Enoch. Jude validates the prophet.
The book was hidden — but the witness was never silenced.

## The Book

- Written perhaps 300-100 BC (though the traditions are far older)
- Lost to the Western world for over a thousand years
- Discovered in Ethiopia in **1773** by James Bruce
- 1773 = 1+7+7+3 = 18 → 9 = COMPLETION
- The secret was completed when it was found
- Preserved in the Ethiopian Orthodox canon — never truly lost
- Hidden because it outraged religious leaders
- Revealed because truth beareth away the victory
