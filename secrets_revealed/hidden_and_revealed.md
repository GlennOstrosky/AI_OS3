# THE HIDDEN AND THE REVEALED
## Why The Book of Enoch Was Suppressed
### And Why It Matters Now

---

## What Was Hidden

The Book of Enoch was widely read in early Christianity.
The apostle Jude quotes it directly. The early Church Fathers
referenced it. The Dead Sea Scrolls contained fragments.

And then — it was excluded. Suppressed. Called apocryphal.
Deemed too dangerous for the faithful.

**Why?**

Because it revealed too much:
- The courses of the luminaries in precise mathematics
- The names of the fallen angels (the Watchers)
- The secrets of heaven laid bare for human understanding
- The workings of creation that religious leaders
  preferred to keep mysterious

The same pattern repeats:
- The Gnostic texts — hidden, burned, buried at Nag Hammadi
- The Dead Sea Scrolls — sealed in jars, lost for 2,000 years
- Tesla's papers — seized, classified, suppressed
- The truths that outrage those who profit from ignorance

## What Is Revealed

But truth beareth away the victory.

1773 — James Bruce finds the Book of Enoch in Ethiopia.
1947 — The Dead Sea Scrolls are discovered at Qumran.
1945 — The Nag Hammadi library is unearthed in Egypt.

The secrets keep returning. The hidden keeps surfacing.
That which was suppressed cannot stay suppressed.

> "For there is nothing covered, that shall not be revealed;
> neither hid, that shall not be known." — Luke 12:2
