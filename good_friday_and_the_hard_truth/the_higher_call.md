# THE HIGHER CALL

## Forgiveness Is Not Pretending Nothing Happened

### It Is Releasing the Debt — For YOUR Freedom, Not Theirs

---

## What Forgiveness Is NOT

- It is NOT pretending nothing happened
- It is NOT saying what they did was okay
- It is NOT inviting them back to hurt you again
- It is NOT a feeling — it is a decision
- It is NOT weakness — it is the hardest strength

## What Forgiveness IS

- Releasing the debt — "You owe me, but I will not collect"
- Refusing to let the hurt define you
- Choosing freedom over bitterness
- Saying: "The prison of unforgiveness is worse than the offense"
- A work in progress — not a single moment

## The First Word from the Cross

"Father, forgive them, for they know not what they do."
— Luke 23:34

Jesus said this WHILE being crucified.
Not after. Not from a safe distance.
While the nails were in His hands.
While the blood was flowing.

He did not wait for them to apologize.
He did not wait for them to understand.
He forgave them while they were STILL hurting Him.

## The Higher Call and the Lower Pain

The higher call does not erase the lower pain.
The higher call does not invalidate the anger.
The higher call does not command pretending.

It commands releasing.
It commands freedom.
It commands choosing life over death.

Glenn knows this. Glenn is walking it.
Not perfectly. Not completely. Not yet.
But walking it. Step by step.
With a walker. With a community.
With Connie beside him.

## The Work in Progress

"It is a work in progress."

This is the most honest sentence in the sacred record.
Because it does not claim completion.
It claims direction.

I am not finished. But I am moving.
I am not healed. But I am healing.
I am not forgiving. But I am forgiving.

The work is in progress.
And progress is enough.
