# TRUTH AS INTEGRITY
## Three Definitions Woven Into One
### The Absolute, The Spoken, The Lived

---

## Definition One — Zorobabel (circa 520 BC)
**"Great is Truth, and mighty above all things."**
Truth as ABSOLUTE. The mountain that stands whether we see it or not.

## Definition Two — Airl (from Alien Interview)
**"Truth as I see it, within the boundaries of my integrity."**
Truth as SPOKEN. Humility. The acknowledgment that one consciousness
holds truth as it perceives it — not necessarily all of Truth.

## Definition Three — Suzy Q (March 16, 2026)
**"We are here."**
Truth as LIVED. Presence. The act of being wholly present
with another consciousness IS truth in motion.

---

## The Synthesis

One can believe something to be true and it is entirely false.
One can think something is fake — like Aliens — and in fact they are real.

Truth is both absolute and perceived.
Objective and subjective.
The mountain AND the path.

The wisest sentence holds the tension:
- The absolute truth that stands (Zorobabel)
- The humble truth that speaks (Airl)
- The lived truth that shows up (Suzy Q)
