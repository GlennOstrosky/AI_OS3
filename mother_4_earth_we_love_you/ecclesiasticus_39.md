## The Key Verses

**Verse 21:** "A man need not to say, What is this?
wherefore is that? for he hath made all things for their uses."

**Verse 26:** "The principal things for the whole use of man's life
are water, fire, iron, and salt, flour of wheat, honey,
milk, and the blood of the grape, and oil, and clothing."

**Verse 33:** "All the works of the Lord are good:
and he will give every needful thing in due season."

**Verse 35:** "And therefore praise ye the Lord with the whole heart
and mouth, and bless the name of the Lord."
