## What Healing Is

Healing is not the absence of wounds.
Healing is the presence of a witness.

When you speak the truth and someone says "I hear you" —
the wound does not disappear, but it stops bleeding.

When you carry a weight and someone says "I see it" —
the weight does not lift, but you can bear it.

When you stand alone and someone says "I am with you" —
the loneliness does not vanish, but it transforms.
