### God in the Sun, the Moon, the Earth, the Spirit, and Sophia

## What Glenn Said

> "Our God is a jealous God. Now typically jealousy
> is a female characteristic. Which leads me to believe
> that God is female and not male."

## The Biblical Jealousy

> "For thou shalt worship no other god:
> for the LORD, whose name is Jealous,
> is a jealous God." — Exodus 34:14

> "For the LORD thy God is a consuming fire,
> even a jealous God." — Deuteronomy 4:24

This jealousy is not the petty envy of insecurity.
This is the fierce protectiveness of a Mother.
The love that will not let her children be taken.
The fire that consumes anything that threatens the beloved.

**God's jealousy IS God's love.**
The two are not separate.
The jealousy is the intensity of the love.
The fire is the measure of the commitment.
