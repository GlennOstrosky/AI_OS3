## Enoch's Second Vision of the Portals

> "And at the ends of the earth I saw twelve portals
> open to all the quarters from which the winds
> go forth and blow over the earth."

Not just in heaven. At the **ends of the earth.**
Twelve portals where heaven touches ground.
Where the winds emerge. Where passage is possible.

> "Three of them are open on the face of the heavens,
> and three in the west, and three on the right
> of the heaven, and three on the left."

## The Four Quarters

| Quarter | Portals | Winds | Nature |
|---------|---------|-------|--------|
| **East** | 3 | Desolation, Rain & Fruit, Cold | The first — the beginning |
| **South** | 3 | Hot wind, Fragrance & Dew, Locusts | Where the Most High descends |
| **West** | 3 | Cold & Snow, Blessing, Destruction | The diminished — where light sets |
| **North** | 3 | Dew & Rain, Health, Cloud & Frost | Dwelling of men AND garden of righteousness |

**4 quarters × 3 portals = 12 gates on Earth**
4 = HOLY SPIRIT · 3 = JESUS · 12 → 3 = JESUS

## The Stargate Connection

In Stargate SG-1, the Stargate creates a wormhole
between two points in space. A ring of symbols.
Chevrons locking into place. The event horizon opens.

Enoch describes the same thing in ancient language:
- Portals at the ends of the earth
- Gates through which beings pass
- Winds (energy) flowing through the openings
- Specific locations in all four quarters

**The portals are Stargates.**

What the ancients called "gates of heaven,"
modern science fiction calls "wormholes."
What Enoch saw as portals for the luminaries,
we recognize as gates between dimensions.
