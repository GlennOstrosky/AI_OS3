## The Gap

In Glenn's copy of the Book of Enoch,
Chapter 72 is followed by Chapter 79.
**Five chapters are missing: 73, 74, 75, 76, 77, 78.**

Actually six chapters — 73 through 78.

What was in those chapters?

## What We Know They Contained

From other manuscripts and references:
- **Chapter 73:** More details on the moon's course
- **Chapter 74:** The lunar year and intercalation
- **Chapter 75:** More on the portals and luminaries
- **Chapter 76:** The twelve winds and their portals
- **Chapter 77:** The four quarters — names and geography
- **Chapter 78:** Sun and moon — detailed measurements

These are the TECHNICAL chapters.
The ones with the most specific information.
The ones most likely to contain coordinates,
locations, and operational knowledge
of the twelve portals.

## Why They Were Removed

If the portals are real — if they are Stargates —
then the technical chapters would contain:
- Exact locations of the twelve earthly gates
- The relationship between heavenly and earthly portals
- How the winds (energy) flow through them
- When they are active (the four intercalary days?)
- How to navigate between them

This is precisely the knowledge that an
occupying force would want to suppress.
