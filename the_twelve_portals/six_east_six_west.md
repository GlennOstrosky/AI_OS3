# SIX EAST SIX WEST
## The Twelve Portals of Heaven
### As Uriel Showed Enoch — Chapter 72

---

## The Vision

> "And I saw six portals in which the sun rises,
> and six portals in which the sun sets
> and the moon rises and sets in these portals,
> and the leaders of the stars and those whom they lead:
> six in the east and six in the west,
> and all following each other in accurately corresponding order."

Six gates of sunrise. Six gates of sunset.
Twelve portals in the heavens through which
Brother Sun and Sister Moon travel their courses.

## The Geography of Heaven

| Direction | Portals | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| East | 6 | Where the sun RISES — the gates of beginning |
| West | 6 | Where the sun SETS — the gates of return |
| **Total** | **12** | **12 → 3 = JESUS holds the gates** |

## The Sun's Path Through the Portals

The sun does not rise from the same gate each day.
He moves through the portals as the seasons turn:

- **Portal 4** → Spring begins: day 10 parts, night 8
- **Portal 5** → Spring deepens: day 11 parts, night 7
- **Portal 6** → Summer peak: day 12 parts, night 6 (day doubles night)
- Returns through **6 → 5 → 4 → 3 → 2 → 1** as winter approaches
- **Portal 1** → Winter depth: night 12 parts, day 6 (night doubles day)

The dance of light and darkness.
The annual rhythm of the luminaries.
Every portal a gate. Every gate a season.

## The Sacred Numbers

6 + 6 = 12 → 3 = JESUS — the Son holds the portals
12 portals = 12 months = 12 tribes = 12 apostles = 12 Council seats
4 quarters × 3 portals each = the Foundation × the Son
