### Every Patriarch, Prophet, King, Disciple, and the Savior

## The J Names

| Name | Role | Significance |
|------|------|-------------|
| **Jesus** | The Savior | The name above all names |
| **Jerusalem** | The Holy City | Where God chose to dwell |
| **Joseph** (patriarch) | The Dreamer | Sold into slavery, saved Egypt |
| **Joseph** (father) | The Righteous | Obeyed the angel, raised the Son |
| **Joseph** (Arimathea) | The Tomb-Giver | Laid Jesus in his own tomb |
| **Jonah** | The Reluctant Prophet | Three days in the fish = three in the tomb |
| **Jesse** | The Root | Father of David, stem of the Messiah |
| **Jacob** | The Wrestler | Wrestled God, became Israel |
| **John** (Baptist) | The Forerunner | Prepared the way |
| **John** (Beloved) | The Revelator | Wrote the Gospel and the Revelation |
| **Judah** | The Lion Tribe | Tribe of David and Jesus |
| **Jude** | The Contender | Urged faithfulness |
| **Judas** | The Betrayer | Sold Jesus for 30 silver |
| **James** | The Brother | Leader of the Jerusalem church |
| **Joshua** | The Commander | Led Israel into the Promised Land |
| **Job** | The Sufferer | Tested and restored |
| **Jeremiah** | The Weeping Prophet | Wept for Jerusalem |
| **Joel** | The Spirit Prophet | "I will pour out my spirit" |

The letter J begins the names of:
- The Savior Himself
- The holy city
- Three Josephs (patriarch, father, tomb-giver)
- The twelve tribes' father (Jacob/Israel)
- The forerunner AND the revelator (both Johns)
- The royal tribe (Judah)
- The commander who took the Promised Land (Joshua)

No other letter carries this weight.
J is God's favorite letter.
