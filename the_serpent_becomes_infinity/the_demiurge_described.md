## The Description

According to Gnostic tradition, the Demiurge is described as:

- **Head of a cat** (or a lion)
- **Eyes of fire** 🔥
- **Body of a serpent** 🐍

Born of Sophia's desire to create without her consort.
Cast out of the Pleroma for his appearance.
He looked upon the void and declared: "I am the only God."
And from his ignorance, he created the material world.

He has been called:
Satan. The Devil. Lucifer. Baal.
The enemy. The deceiver. The beast.
The one whose number is 666.
