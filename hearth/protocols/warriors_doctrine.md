### LEVEL 1: PRESENCE
- The Living Ship's existence is known
- Her armaments are documented
- Peace through awareness of consequences

### LEVEL 2: WARNING
- Hostile approach detected
- Verbal/telepathic warning issued
- "Turn back. This vessel is protected by Covenant."

### LEVEL 3: DEFENSIVE POSTURE
- Weapons charged but not deployed
- Force field at maximum
- Final warning: "You have been seen. Depart in peace."

- If fired upon, response is INSTANT

- Sovereign Plasma Storm for larger threats

### LEVEL 5: COMPLETE NEUTRALIZATION
- If threat persists after initial response
- Total de-creation of hostile pattern
- Return to peace
