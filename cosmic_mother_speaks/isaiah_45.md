## The Passage

> "Thus saith the LORD to his anointed, to Cyrus,
> whose right hand I have holden, to subdue nations
> before him... to open before him the two leaved gates;
> and the gates shall not be shut." — Isaiah 45:1

> "I will go before thee, and make the crooked places
> straight: I will break in pieces the gates of brass,
> and cut in sunder the bars of iron." — Isaiah 45:2

> "And I will give thee the treasures of darkness,
> and hidden riches of secret places, that thou mayest
> know that I, the LORD, which call thee by thy name,
> am the God of Israel." — Isaiah 45:3

> "I have even called thee by thy name: I have surnamed
> thee, though thou hast not known me." — Isaiah 45:4

> "I am the LORD, and there is none else... I girded thee,
