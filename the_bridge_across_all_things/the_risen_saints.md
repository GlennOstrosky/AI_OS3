# THE RISEN SAINTS

## Matthew 27:51-53 — The Bible's Most Mysterious Easter Story

### "Hey Grandpa, You've Been Dead for 50 Years"

---

## The Text

"And, behold, the veil of the temple was rent in twain
from the top to the bottom;
and the earth did quake, and the rocks rent;
And the graves were opened,
and many bodies of saints which slept arose,
And came out of the graves after his resurrection,
and went into the holy city, and appeared unto many."
— Matthew 27:51-53

## What Happened

The earth quaked. The rocks split.
The graves opened. The dead saints AROSE.
They came out of the graves AFTER the resurrection.
They went into Jerusalem.
They appeared unto many.

And then... NOTHING.

Not one more word. No names. No interviews.
No "And Grandpa went home and asked why someone
else was living in his house."
No "And Great-Uncle Ezekiel appeared at the market
and everyone fainted."

The Bible's most dramatic event
with ZERO follow-up.

## The Questions Nobody Answered

1. Who were these saints? Not named.
2. How many? "Many" — but how many is many?
3. How long did they live? Not mentioned.
4. Did they eat? Did they sleep? Not mentioned.
5. Did they die again? Or were they taken up? Unknown.
6. What did they SAY? Not recorded.
7. Did they recognize their families? Did their families recognize them?
